Marketing Manager – Key Responsibilities

Develop, implement, and manage comprehensive marketing strategies to build brand awareness and drive growth.

Plan and oversee campaigns across digital, social, email, and offline channels to ensure consistency and impact.

Conduct market research to identify new opportunities, customer preferences, and competitive insights.

Collaborate with cross-functional teams (sales, product, design, etc.) to align marketing strategies with business goals.

Manage and optimize the company’s online presence, including website content, SEO, and social media.

Track, measure, and report on the performance of marketing campaigns against KPIs and ROI.

Lead, mentor, and develop the marketing team to achieve department objectives.

Build and maintain strong relationships with external partners, media agencies, and vendors.

Monitor industry trends, customer behavior, and emerging technologies to recommend innovative approaches.

Manage the marketing budget effectively to maximize impact and efficiency.
